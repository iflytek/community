This page tracks liaisons between the Iflytek Opensource Community and external organizations, projects, or other communities.

## Purpose

Liaisons serve as points of contact and coordination between communities. They help:

* Facilitate communication and collaboration
* Share best practices and learnings
* Coordinate on cross-project initiatives
* Represent the community in external forums

## Becoming a Liaison

If you're interested in serving as a liaison between our community and another organization, please:

1. Discuss with the relevant SIG leads
2. File an issue proposing the liaison relationship
3. Include your background and the value this relationship would bring
