These values guide our work as a community.

## Be Welcoming

We strive to be approachable and friendly. We want new contributors to feel included and empowered to participate.

## Be Open

We prefer transparency. Decisions, discussions, and designs should happen in public forums whenever possible. This ensures that anyone can participate and learn, not just those in the inner circle.

## Be Respectful

We value diverse perspectives and treat everyone with respect. Disagreements happen, but we handle them constructively and professionally.

## Focus on Impact

We prioritize work that has the greatest positive impact on users and the community. We value substance over style and results over process.

## Grow Together

We invest in mentoring, documentation, and processes that help new contributors succeed. We celebrate contributions of all kinds, not just code.

## Be Decisive

We value making decisions and taking action over endless discussion. We recognize that perfect is the enemy of good, and that we can often iterate to improve.

## Lead by Example

Community leaders are expected to model these values in their interactions and decisions.
